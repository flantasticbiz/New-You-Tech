ABOUT YOU-TECH AFRICA

Raising Africa's Next Generation
of Tech Innovators

At You-Tech Africa, we believe every young African deserves access to opportunities that can transform their future through technology. We are building a generation of skilled, confident, and innovative young people equipped to thrive in the digital economy.

WHO WE ARE

You-Tech Africa is a tech-driven capacity-building organisation

focused on equipping young Africans with the skills, experience, and support they need to succeed in today's digital world.

We go beyond traditional learning by combining practical training, mentorship, and real-world projects to prepare you for actual career opportunities in tech.

  • Practical hands-on training in UI/UX, Frontend, and Product Management.
  • Direct mentorship from industry experts at global tech companies.
  • Real-world project experience that builds professional portfolios.

Our Vision

A continent where every young person has access to high-quality tech education, mentorship, and opportunities to innovate, lead, and create sustainable impact through technology.

Our Mission

To empower young Africans with practical tech skills, leadership development, and career-ready experiences that transform lives, communities, and futures.

THE VALUES THAT DRIVE US

Everything we do at You-Tech Africa is built on a foundation of growth, innovation, and impact.

Excellence

Maintaining high standards in training and mentorship to build stellar careers.

Innovation

Promoting creativity and problem-solving to keep our curriculum ahead of the curve.

Impact

Focusing on measurable change with outcomes that transform individual lives.

Collaboration

Building strong networks and partnerships because growth happens together.

Everything you need to know about getting started
