Everything you need to know about getting started

You-Tech Africa is open to young Africans between the ages of 16 and 25 who are passionate about learning tech skills, growing their careers, and gaining real-world experience.

No, prior experience is not required. Our programs are beginner-friendly and designed to guide learners from the fundamentals to practical application.

We offer training in Product Design, Frontend Development, Data Analysis, Product Management and other in-demand tech skills focused on preparing learners for the digital economy.

Yes. We provide mentorship, coaching, and internship opportunities where learners work on real-world projects and receive guidance from experienced professionals.

Our programs are scholarship-based and completely free. However, we require a small commitment fee to encourage accountability and active participation from learners throughout the programme. Full details are usually shared during each cohort application process.
